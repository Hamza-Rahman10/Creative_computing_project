Max Slinger : The video Game

Game Description


Max Slinger was once a regular guy in a world where peace was the norm, where the city thrived under clear skies and bustling streets. But now, that world has been invaded by ruthless aliens, turning everything upside down. Max, an ordinary man pushed into extraordinary circumstances, finds himself at the forefront of the battle to save his city. With courage as his weapon and determination as his shield, Max steps up to face the alien threat head-on. Despite the chaos and destruction around him, he refuses to give up on his home. Max Slinger, once an everyday citizen, is now the last hope for his city's survival. Take back what was once rightfully yours

Game Controls


  • Move Forward: W
  • Move Back: S
  • Shoot: Left Mouse Button
  • R: Restart
